Policy

Relay rules

Edit this file to describe the requirements for participating servers.

Participation

  • Participating servers must comply with applicable law.
  • Do not use the relay for spam, harassment, malware, or coordinated abuse.
  • Operators must maintain a working abuse or administrative contact.
  • Servers that create excessive technical load may be limited or removed.

Moderation

The relay operator may suspend or remove a server when necessary to protect users, infrastructure, or other participating communities. Decisions are made on a best-effort basis and may be revisited when new information is available.

Availability

The relay is provided without a service-level guarantee. Maintenance, security events, software defects, or resource constraints may interrupt service.
